What is Crochet???

Crocheting is a hand crafted work in which is made up into a patterned fabric by looping yarn/wool with a hooked needle (hook).
WARNING:Crocheting may seem and look easy but I assure you that it takes lots of time and paience.

Basic Stiches Of Crochet

  • Slip Stich (ss)
  • Single Crochet (sc)
  • Double Crochet (dc)
  • Triple Crochet (tc)

Facts About Crocheting

Here are some facts about crocheting that I though were kinda Intresting:
  1. Crochet helped pull the Irish out of the depths of the devastating potato famine (1845–1850). A group of Ursuline nuns taught local women thread crochet, which became known as "Irish Crochet." It became incredibly popular and provided much-needed income.
  2. Over 2,000 women from 14 countries met in India to create the world's largest crocheted blanket. The mega-blanket measured approximately 120,000 square feet, or about the size of one-and-a-half football fields.
  3. Crocheting has been proven to help with insomnia, stress, anxiety, and depression.
  4. Crocheting can reduce the risk of Alzheimer's by 30–50%.
